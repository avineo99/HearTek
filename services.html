Advanced Hearing Assessments

Comprehensive Hearing Tests in Melbourne

Our comprehensive hearing assessments go beyond basic screenings to accurately diagnose the type, severity, and impact of your hearing loss.

What’s Included in an Advanced Hearing Test?

  • Detailed Consultation – Discuss your hearing history, symptoms, and lifestyle factors.
  • Otoscopy (Ear Examination) –Checks for blockages, infections, or abnormalities in the ear canal.
  • Pure Tone Audiometry –Identifies the quietest sounds you can hear across different frequencies.
  • Air & Bone Conduction Testing – Determines if your hearing loss is conductive, sensorineural, or mixed.
  • Speech Audiometry – Measures how well you understand speech clarity in various environments.
  • Middle Ear Function Testing (Tympanometry) –Detects fluid buildup, blockages, or eardrum issues.
  • Results Review & Next Steps – Your audiologist will explain your test results and suggest treatment options.

Why Choose an Advanced Hearing Assessment?

  •  Accurate Diagnosis – Pinpoints the specific cause and type of hearing loss.
  •  Personalized Treatment Plan – Tailored hearing aid or assistive listening device recommendations.
  •  Early Detection of Hearing Disorders – Can identify conditions like Ménière’s disease, otosclerosis, and auditory processing disorders.

Professional Ear Wax Removal

Safe & Effective Ear Cleaning

Earwax, also known as cerumen, plays an essential role in protecting and lubricating the ear canal. However, excessive buildup can cause hearing difficulties, discomfort, and even infections. Our professional earwax removal services ensure safe and effective cleaning to restore optimal hearing.

We utilize advanced micro-suction technology to safely remove earwax without causing damage to the ear canal. Unlike cotton swabs or home remedies, our professional methods effectively clear blockages without pushing wax deeper into the ear.

Signs You May Need Earwax Removal

  • Muffled or reduced hearing
  • A sensation of fullness or blockage in the ear
  • Ringing in the ears (tinnitus)
  • Ear pain or discomfort
  • Frequent ear infections

Why Choose Professional Earwax Removal?

  • Safe and painless –Our methods minimize the risk of injury or irritation.
  • Quick and effective –The procedure is completed in minutes with immediate relief.
  • No risk of damage –Unlike DIY solutions, professional removal prevents pushing wax further into the canal.

Assistive Listening Devices

Enhancing Sound Clarity

Hearing aids are not the only solution for those experiencing hearing difficulties. Assistive listening devices (ALDs) provide additional support in specific environments, ensuring clearer communication and improved hearing in daily life. Our selection of assistive listening devices includes:

Types of Assistive Listening Devices:

  • TV Amplifiers – Enhances sound clarity from televisions, allowing users to enjoy programs at a comfortable volume without disturbing others.
  • Personal FM Systems – Wireless systems that transmit a speaker’s voice directly to the listener, ideal for classrooms, lecture halls, and large meetings.
  • Telephone Amplifiers – Devices that boost phone call volume and clarity, making conversations easier for individuals with hearing loss.
  • Loop Systems – Designed for public spaces such as theaters and churches, these systems send sound directly to hearing aids equipped with a telecoil feature.
  • Alerting Devices – Visual or vibrating alerts for doorbells, alarms, and phone calls, ensuring important sounds are never missed.
